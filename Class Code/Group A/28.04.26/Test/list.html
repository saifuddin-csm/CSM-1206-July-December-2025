Bangladesh Agricultural University (BAU)

Bangladesh Agricultural University (BAU), located in Mymensingh, is the leading institution for agricultural education and research in Bangladesh. Established in 1961, BAU plays a vital role in developing skilled professionals, conducting innovative research, and contributing to national food security and sustainable agricultural development.

Faculties (Unordered List)

  • Agriculture
  • Animal Husbandry
  • Fisheries
  • Agricultural Engineering and Technology (AET)
  • Veterinary Science
  • Agricultural Economics and Rural Sociology

Degrees Offered (Ordered List)

  1. Bachelor of Science (BSc)
  2. Master of Science (MS)
  3. Doctor of Philosophy (PhD)

Achievements (Description List)

Research Excellence
BAU has produced high-impact agricultural research recognized nationally and internationally.
Skilled Graduates
Graduates from BAU are serving in government, academia, and industry worldwide.
National Contribution
BAU significantly contributes to food security, livestock development, and fisheries management.
